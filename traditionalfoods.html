Pad Thai

Pad thai, phat thai, or phad thai, is a stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food in Thailand as part of the country's cuisine.

Tom Yum Soup

Tom yum or tom yam is a type of hot and sour Thai soup, usually cooked with shrimp. The words "tom yam" are derived from two Thai words.

Khao Soi

Khao soi or khao soy is a Chin Haw dish served in Laos and northern Thailand. A comparable dish, ohn no khao swè, is widely served in Myanmar.

Som Tam

Image result Som tam is a Thai papaya salad. Don't confuse Thai salads with the boring leafy salads the rest of the world enjoys.Som Tam is served in restaurants and by vendors.

Massaman Curry

Massaman curry is a rich, flavourful, and mildly spicy Thai curry. It is a fusion dish, combining ingredients from Persia,the Indian subcontineny and the Malay Archipelago

Mango Sticky Rice

Mango sticky rice is a traditional Southeast Asian and South Asian dessert made with glutinous rice, fresh mango and coconut milk, and eaten with a spoon or the hands.

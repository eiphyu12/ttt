When is the best time to visit Thailand?

The best time to visit Thailand is during high season which takes place between November to February, when the weather is generally at its best throughout the country. In northern Thailand dry season is between November & May; June to October is characterised by heavy rainfall and temperatures are cooler between October & January. The south can be split into two: the wettest months on the west coast are between April & October; whilst the east coast experiences heavy rainfall from September to December. During the rest of the year, dry and sunny weather is the order of the day. If you want to know more about the weather you can go and check this link www.selectiveasia.com/thailand-holidays/weather.
